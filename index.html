hi! i'm kaitlyn radde.
about me

i live in washington, d.c. and currently work as a data reporting intern at chalkbeat.

before that, i studied quantitative political science at indiana university. at iu, i worked as an enterprise editor and reporter at the indiana daily student and a producer at wfiu, bloomington's npr affiliate station.

i like using data and documents to tell compelling stories that hold powerful people accountable. because of that, i've covered a wide array of institutions, including a police department, a hospital, a prison system, and indiana university.

want to chat? you can reach me via email or find me on twitter and linkedin! in the meantime, you can see some of my best work below.
