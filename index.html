about me

i live in washington, d.c. and am currently working as npr’s newsapps intern.

before that, i interned with chalkbeat and usa today's data teams. in college, i studied quantitative political science at indiana university, where i worked as an enterprise editor and reporter at the indiana daily student.

i like using data and documents to tell compelling stories that hold powerful people accountable. because of that, i've covered a wide array of institutions, including a police department, a hospital, a prison system, and indiana university. i'm more interested in getting and digging into data than any specific beat, but I’m particularly interested in using my skills to cover issues related to incarceration and health care.

want to chat? you can reach me via email or find me on twitter and linkedin! in the meantime, you can see some of my best work below and my resume here.
